The Beginning

A Recipe Born in a
Village Kitchen

It started in a small village in Tamil Nadu, where our grandmother — lovingly called Ammachi — would wake before dawn to grind fresh spices for the family's daily cooking. Her masala powders were legendary in the neighbourhood, and neighbours would always ask for a small portion.

"When you grind spices yourself, you can smell whether they're right. A machine can't do that. Your hands know."

Decades later, we're carrying that same philosophy into a modern world. Every powder we make follows her original recipes exactly — the same ratios, the same roasting times, the same love.

We started SPICE in 2019 to share these flavours with families who miss the taste of home-ground spices — and to preserve a culinary heritage that deserves to live on.

Our Process

From Farm to Your
Kitchen Shelf

🌾

Source & Select

We personally select whole spices from trusted farmers — Erode turmeric, Guntur chillies, Idukki pepper, and locally grown curry leaves. Only the best batches make the cut.

☀️

Sun Dry & Clean

All ingredients are spread on clean cloth and sun-dried for 2–3 days to reduce moisture. They're then hand-cleaned to remove any impurities before roasting.

🔥

Slow Roast

Each spice is roasted separately on a low flame in a heavy iron pan — a technique that develops deep flavour notes without burning the volatile aromatic oils.

🪨

Stone Grind

We use a traditional wet-grinder converted for dry grinding — the slow, cool grinding process that commercial facilities can't replicate. This preserves every molecule of flavour.

📦

Pack & Deliver

Freshly ground powder is cooled to room temperature then sealed in food-grade airtight pouches with manufacture date. Delivered within 24 hours of packing.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long do the powders stay fresh? ▾
Stored in an airtight container away from direct sunlight, our spice powders stay fresh for 3–4 months. For best flavour, use within 2 months of opening.
Can I customise spice level or quantity? ▾
Absolutely! Just mention your preference when you WhatsApp us — extra spicy, mild, or any custom combination. We also take bulk orders for events and gifting.
Do you use any preservatives? ▾
Never. All our powders contain 100% natural ingredients with no preservatives, artificial colours, or flavour enhancers. The salt content itself acts as a natural preservative.
How do I place an order? ▾
It's easy! Just click the WhatsApp button, tell us what you need (product, weight, quantity), share your delivery address, and we'll confirm your order and payment details.
What payment methods do you accept? ▾
We accept UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), bank transfer, and cash on delivery within Chennai. Payment is made after order confirmation.
